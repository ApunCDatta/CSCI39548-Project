Our Sweet Story

From Our Family Kitchen to Yours

What began in 2010 as a few cherished recipes in our family's city kitchen has blossomed into Sugar & Spice. We believe every treat should be a warm hug for the soul.

By pairing the velvety richness of world-class Belgian chocolate with the vibrant, sun-ripened flavors of locally sourced organic fruits, we handcraft small-batch delights that honor our roots while celebrating the best of nature.
